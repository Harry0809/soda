Upcoming artists.

MR. EVERYBODY

Despite his now huge global following, celebrity clientele and worldwide solo exhibitions, Mr. Everybody's street art roots can still be seen in his work today. His unique style offers up a clash of bleak imagery and playful vibrancy. The work, often minimalist in execution, draws on both street art and classical influences, with elegant figures and pop culture iconography playing an important role. To say we're excited about working with him would be a huge understatement.

ALEJANDRA SIEDER

Multi-award-winning visual artist, Alejandra Sieder is a force to be reckoned with. A graduate of the Miguel Neumann Design Institute in Venezuela, she now lives and works in Sydney Australia. With her latest body of work she aims to engage the audience by challenging and pushing the limits of their comfort zones. We're delighted to be working with her as she takes her first steps into the world of NFTs.

TERRY CHISHOLM

From producing work for some of the world's biggest brands to hosting exhibitions around the globe, Terry has enjoyed a long and illustrious career as an art director, graphic designer and artist. His work has been recognised at a host of international shows and appeared in numerous publications, from D&AD to Semi Permanent. Sometimes playful, sometimes downright dark but always unique, he never fails to surprise and delight. We're very excited (and a little scared!) to be joining him on his next adventure.

Work with us.

We love to collaborate. Whether we're digitising sculptures, turning brush strokes into code or NFTising* paintings, we exist to help creators bring their visions to life on the blockchain. So, if you're an artist considering stepping into the world of NFTs, don't hesitate to get in touch.
